This trend indicates a strategic focus on expanding its presence in North America and diversifying its revenue sources.

Key products and services driving growth include corporate banking, investment banking, and payment solutions, particularly in the North American and European markets. The bank's ability to leverage its global footprint allows it to tap into various markets, enhancing its revenue potential.
