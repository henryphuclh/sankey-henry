Revenue Drivers

TJX Companies Inc. operates primarily through four business segments: Marmaxx, HomeGoods, TJX Canada, and TJX International. For FY2026, total revenue reached $60.37 billion, with Marmaxx being the largest contributor at $36.59 billion, accounting for approximately 60.7% of total revenue. HomeGoods followed with $10.17 billion (16.9%), TJX Canada contributed $5.63 billion (9.3%), and TJX International added $7.99 billion (13.2%).

Over the past three years, TJX has experienced consistent revenue growth across its segments. In FY2024, total revenue was $54.22 billion, which increased to $56.36 billion in FY2025, and further to $60.37 billion in FY2026, representing a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of approximately 5.5%. The Marmaxx segment has shown the most significant growth, increasing from $33.41 billion in FY2024 to $36.59 billion in FY2026, a growth of 9.5%. HomeGoods also demonstrated strong performance, growing from $8.99 billion to $10.17 billion over the same period, reflecting a 13.1% increase.

The revenue mix has evolved, with Marmaxx maintaining its dominance while HomeGoods has gained a larger share of total revenue. This shift indicates a growing consumer preference for home-related products, which has been a key driver of growth for TJX.
