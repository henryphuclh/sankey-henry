The relatively high SG&A expenses are typical for pharmaceutical companies, reflecting the costs associated with marketing and distribution.

Operating leverage is evident in Novo Nordisk's financial performance, as the company has maintained stable revenue while effectively managing its cost structure. The consistent gross and operating margins suggest that the company is well-positioned to absorb fluctuations in revenue without significantly impacting profitability.
