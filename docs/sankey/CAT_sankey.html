Revenue Drivers

Caterpillar Inc. operates through four primary business segments: Construction Industries, Resource Industries, Power & Energy, and Financial Products Segment. As of FY2025, the total revenue reached $67.59 billion, with the following contributions from each segment:

  • Construction Industries: $22.93 billion (33.9%)
  • Resource Industries: $11.42 billion (16.9%)
  • Power & Energy: $29.38 billion (43.4%)
  • Financial Products Segment: $3.86 billion (5.7%)

Over the past three years, the revenue mix has shown some fluctuations. The Construction Industries segment has remained relatively stable, contributing approximately $22.51 billion in FY2023, $23.32 billion in FY2024, and $22.93 billion in FY2025. The Resource Industries segment has also shown slight growth, increasing from $11.15 billion in FY2023 to $11.42 billion in FY2025.

The Power & Energy segment has been a significant driver of growth, with revenues increasing from $22.99 billion in FY2023 to $29.38 billion in FY2025, reflecting a strategic focus on energy solutions. The Financial Products Segment has also seen growth, from $3.11 billion in FY2023 to $3.86 billion in FY2025, indicating a diversification of revenue sources.

Key products driving growth include construction and mining equipment, energy solutions, and financial services, with geographic expansion in emerging markets contributing to overall revenue increases.

The cost of goods sold (COGS) is implied in the gross profit figures, which indicate that the company maintains a healthy gross profit margin despite fluctuations in revenue.
